Grid Mode Features

Single Click Fill: Click to fill or unfill a unit on the grid.

Drag Fill: Hold the mouse down and drag to fill many units at once.

Double Click Line Creation: First double click specifies start, second double click specifies end. Cancels line creation if start and end are same.

Sketch Mode Features

Single Click Ellipse Creation: Click three times to create an ellipse. First click specifies center. Distance between first click and second click specifies x-radius. Distance between first click and third click specifies y-radius. Three clicks at same position cancels ellipse.

Double Click Curve Creation: Double click four times to create a simple, bezier curve. First and fourth clicks specify start and end. Second and third clicks specify tangent lines. Cancels curve creation if first and second click are same.
